![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/image-401.png)

1. [Update your graphics card driver](#m1)

Here’s how to do it:

 1) Press the**Windows logo key** and**I** at the same time to invoke the**Settings** app.

 2) Click**System** .
